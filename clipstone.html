FAQ

Important product details.

Clear answers for users, testers, and development handoff.

Does Clip Stone work without internet?

Yes. Clipboard history, search, edit, delete, and local storage work offline. Internet is only needed for online features such as ads.

Does the app read clipboard in the background?

No. Modern Android restricts background clipboard access. Clip Stone checks clipboard content when the user opens or returns to the app.

Where is clipboard data stored?

Clip data is stored locally using Room Database, which is built on SQLite.

Will ads interrupt copying?

No. The monetization plan avoids launch interstitials and avoids interrupting the copy-paste loop.

What devices are targeted?

The product is optimized for low-end Android devices with 2GB–4GB RAM while still feeling polished on higher-end phones.
