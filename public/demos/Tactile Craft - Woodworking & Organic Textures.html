About Us

At Tactile Craft, we blend traditional woodworking techniques with modern design, creating pieces that feel alive and organic. Our focus is on deep forest greens, warm oaks, and charcoal palettes to evoke a natural, tactile experience.

Features

Experience scroll-driven parallax with moving leaf shadows and wood grains. Our content cards feature a carved wood look with inset shadows, and buttons provide haptic feedback on interaction.
