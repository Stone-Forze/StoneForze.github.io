ARCHAEOLOGICAL HISTORY

Preservation Philosophy

Each artifact in our collection undergoes meticulous conservation using both traditional techniques and cutting-edge technology. Our team of conservators works to stabilize, document, and preserve these pieces for future generations while maintaining historical integrity.

Provenance Research

We maintain complete documentation of each artifact's history, from archaeological excavation to current ownership. Our provenance research follows strict ethical guidelines to ensure all items were legally acquired and properly documented.

Digital Archaeology

Utilizing 3D scanning, photogrammetry, and virtual reality, we create digital twins of our artifacts. This allows for detailed study, virtual exhibitions, and preservation of objects too fragile for regular display.
