WHO WE ARE

We are digital architects obsessed with the raw, unfiltered aesthetic of Neo-Brutalism. We reject the smooth, the polished, the gradient-laden mediocrity of corporate design.

Our work is loud, unapologetic, and built on the foundation of pure geometric forms, high-contrast color theory, and mechanical interaction design. Every pixel serves a purpose. Every border screams intention.

We build experiences that challenge convention, demand attention, and leave lasting impressions in the minds of users who are tired of the same-old design patterns.
